Personal Information

Hello! I'm Rishika Cilji Saravanan, a passionate tech enthusiast and a sophomore in Bachelor of Technology specialized in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning at VIT Chennai. In 11th grade, I chose the Computer Science (CSC) branch, and that's when my journey into the fundamentals of this field truly began. Since then, I have been fascinated by how things work behind the scenes in the digital world, which led me to the path of coding, problem-solving, and technology innovation.

My Journey

My journey has been a rollercoaster ride. Initially, pursuing a career in computer science was not my first choice; it was merely a second option. I entered this field with limited knowledge and understanding. However, as I delved deeper into the subjects, my initial uncertainty transformed into genuine curiosity. I discovered that learning about computer science was like solving intricate puzzles, but with a more realistic approach to addressing real-world problems. This newfound passion has fueled my desire to explore and innovate within the tech industry.
